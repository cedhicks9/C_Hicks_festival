Festival Activities

Day 1

Kids carnival, featuring bounce houses, face painting, petting zoo, carnival games, food trucks, cotton candy, popcorn.

Day 2

Poetry Slam featuring Bay Area poets, Bay Area vendors selling jewelery, clothes, Art made by Bay Area artist. Jazz concert featuring Bay area bands

Day 3

Bay Area rap artist concert. Speeches from Bay Area community leaders.

tickets for festival $15 dollars per day
