Spread Love Spread Food

In response to the hunger crisis in the bay area where some 870,000 people face food insecurity. The Spread love Spread food organization in conjunction with The Peoples Program of Oakland have created a festival to support the free breakfast program and the Bi monthly grocery program. All of the proceeds to this festival will be donated to the Peoples program. This will be a three day event starting December 8th and concluding on December 11th. The festival will take place at Lake Merritt in Oakland
